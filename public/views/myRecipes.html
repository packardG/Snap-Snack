Chicken Parmesan

Time: 55 minutes


Difficulty: 2


Ingredients

Description: This is a classic dish featured in restaurants all around the world. A staple for all ages, chicken parm is a delicious meal great for both last minute dinner plans, or planned dinner parties.

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast 1.5lb
  • 2 eggs
  • Bread crumbs 2.5 cups
  • A.P. Flour 1 cup
  • 1lb of spaghetti
  • 1 Jar of store bought tomato sauce
  • Fresh provolone cheese 2 cups
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
Instructions
  • 1. Preheat oven to 375°
  • 2. Dredge each breast in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and coat in the breadcrumbs. Place breaded breasts on lined baking sheet.
  • 3. Get a stock pot of water going on the stovetop. Add a pinch of salt.
  • 4. Top breaded chicken breasts with some sauce and then cheese. Then, in the oven they go for 35min or until cooked throughout.
  • 5. When water is to a rolling boil, add in spaghetti and cook for 8 minutes.
  • 6. Strain pasta when it is al-dente and toss in some oil or butter.
  • 7. When chicken is done, lay a bed of spaghetti on each plate with a bit of sauce and finally the chicken breast.
